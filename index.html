About

I am a PhD student at the Department of Computer Science, University of Oxford. I work on knowledge graphs under the supervision of Prof. Horrocks, Prof. Cuenca Grau and Dr. Kostylev.

I am originally from St. Petersburg, Russia. I did my undergrad in mathematical linguistics at St. Petersburg State University. Then I moved to Germany where I did my European Masters in Computation Logic at TU Dresden, with an exchange semester in Italy's FUB. Before coming to the UK I worked as a researcher at BIOTEC TU Dresden doing biomedical text mining.
